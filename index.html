Overview

The Challenge

A mid-sized CPG company faced mounting pressure to adopt AI tools without a clear understanding of how, when, and why these technologies should be implemented. The organization needed a strategic framework to evaluate AI solutions based on actual business value rather than market hype.

Discovery Phase
Conducted comprehensive interviews with 12 C-level employees and department heads to understand process inefficiencies, technology debt, and operational gaps
Strategic Approach
Developed company-wide AI ethics and compliance protocol, comprehensive training for organizational alignment, and clear roadmap for tool adoption
Outcome
Prepared organization to make informed AI decisions and avoid blind tool adoption under pressure, creating sustainable competitive advantage
Discovery

Operational Gaps

Through extensive stakeholder interviews, we identified five critical operational gaps preventing the organization from effectively leveraging data and technology.

Data Analysis Paralysis
Teams unable to effectively extract insights from available data, leading to delayed decision-making
Manual Process Bottleneck
Heavy reliance on manual workflows creating inefficiency and increased error rates
Cross-Functional Misalignment
Only 10% overlap in competitive landscape identification across departments
Information Silos
Critical data trapped in departmental silos, preventing holistic business view
Document Management
Lack of centralized system for version control, retrieval, and collaboration on critical documents

Solution

Implementation Roadmap

A phased approach to sustainable AI integration, designed to build organizational capability and strategic alignment before technology deployment.

1
Foundation
Goal Alignment + Competitive Landscape Mapping
Established organizational consensus on strategic priorities and competitive positioning, creating baseline understanding of market forces and internal capabilities.
2
Structure
Data Centralization Strategy
Designed infrastructure roadmap to break down information silos and create unified data architecture supporting advanced analytics and AI applications.
3
Training
Internal FAQ Development
Created comprehensive knowledge base and training program to democratize AI understanding across all organizational levels and departments.
4
Execution
Inventory Clean Up + Strategic Projects
Launched pilot initiatives teaching AI fundamentals through hands-on projects, establishing clear evaluation criteria for future tool adoption.

Key Outcomes

  • By addressing foundational process and tech debt issues, many organizational challenges were immediately mitigated—removing the perceived and artificial reliance on AI as a catch-all solution
  • Strategic projects teaching AI fundamentals at individual level, building organizational capability from the ground up
  • Clear pathway for tool adoption based on actual business needs rather than vendor marketing or competitive pressure
  • Organization equipped to evaluate AI tools critically versus blindly, ensuring ROI and strategic alignment on all technology investments
  • Established AI ethics and compliance framework protecting the organization from regulatory and reputational risks
  • Cross-functional alignment on competitive landscape and strategic priorities, reducing organizational friction and accelerating decision-making
